Example:
find . -maxdepth 1 -name 'your_search_pattern*' -delete

Negate files:
find "$HOME"/.ssh -type f ! -name '*.pub' ! -name "config"

Negate files in .git directory (да, эта команда должны быть такой сложной):
find . -path './.git' -prune -o -type f -print

Applying other commands:
find . -type f -exec echo "This is {}" \;

or:
find . -type f | while IFS= read -r file; do
    echo -e "$file:\n"
    cat "$file"
    echo -e "\n"
done
